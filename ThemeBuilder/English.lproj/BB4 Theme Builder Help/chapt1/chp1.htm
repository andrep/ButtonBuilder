Preparing Images

Buttons are made up of three graphics, Left, Middle and Right, This diagram shows how Button Builder creates and displays buttons. The middle section is stretched to fit the width of the button. The left and right hand image should be the same size. The middle graphic should be 1 pixel wide.

Button Themes consist of 4 images and are named accordingly:
preview.png = This file appears in the themes drawer as a preview.
button_left.png = This is the left hand side of the button.
button_middle.png = This is the middle piece of the button.
button_right.png = This is the right hand side of the button.

What you will need.

For BB4 Theme Builder to create your theme you will need: The left and right side of the button. BB Theme builder will create the middle section and the preview automatically for you.

Tip: Make sure the buttons have a transparent background, otherwise the colour overlay feature will not function correctly. Formats that support transparency are .png .tiff and .psd.
